## Policy Landscape Summary

| Policy | Jurisdiction | Status | Focus |
|--------|-------------|--------|-------|
| EU AI Act | EU | In force | Risk-based, broad |
| US Executive Order | US Federal | In force | Compute thresholds, reporting |
| Export Controls | US (extraterritorial) | In force | Hardware restrictions |
| Colorado AI Act | Colorado | Enacted (2026) | Algorithmic discrimination |
| CA SB 1047 | California | **Vetoed** | Frontier AI safety |
| Canada AIDA | Canada | **Stalled** | High-impact systems |
| RSPs | Industry | Voluntary | Capability thresholds |
| AI Safety Institutes | UK, US, Japan+ | Active | Evaluation, research |
| Seoul Declaration | International | Declared | Coordination |

## How to Read Policy Profiles

Each policy includes:
- **Jurisdiction**: Where does it apply?
- **Status**: Proposed / Enacted / In Force / Vetoed
- **Key Provisions**: What does it actually require?
- **Enforcement**: How is it enforced?
- **Effectiveness Assessment**: Is it likely to matter?
