## Overview

This section catalogs **enacted and proposed AI legislation** across jurisdictions. AI governance is evolving rapidly, with major legislation emerging in the EU, US (federal and state), China, and elsewhere.

## Key Trends

1. **Risk-based approaches** — Most legislation uses risk tiers (EU AI Act model)
2. **Sector-specific rules** — Some legislation targets specific domains (healthcare, employment)
3. **Transparency requirements** — Common across jurisdictions
4. **State-level innovation** — US states filling federal gap
