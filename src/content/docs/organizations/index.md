This section profiles organizations working on AI development, AI safety research, and AI governance.

## Frontier AI Labs

Organizations developing the most advanced AI systems:

- [Anthropic](/organizations/anthropic) - AI safety company developing Claude
- [OpenAI](/organizations/openai) - Developer of GPT and ChatGPT
- [Google DeepMind](/organizations/deepmind) - AI research at Google

Organizations primarily focused on AI safety research:

- [MIRI](/organizations/miri) - Machine Intelligence Research Institute
- [ARC](/organizations/arc) - Alignment Research Center
- [Redwood Research](/organizations/redwood) - Empirical AI safety research

## Governance and Policy Organizations

Organizations focused on AI governance and policy:

- [GovAI](/organizations/govai) - Centre for the Governance of AI
- [CAIS](/organizations/cais) - Center for AI Safety

## Academic Research Groups

University-based AI safety research:
